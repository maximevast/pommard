pommard.sh

Taste a GitHub repository like a fine wine.

A tiny command-line sommelier. Point it at any repo and it pours a five-part tasting card — age, languages, polish, activity, reach — and a weighted score out of 100.

$ curl -fsSL https://pommard.sh/install.sh | bash

Five sips, one verdict

Every card reads the same five things, in the language of the table.

🍇

Millésime

The vintage — how old the repo is, from its very first commit.

Jeune · En développement · En pleine maturité · Grand âge

🎨

Robe

The colour — primary languages and the heft of the codebase.

Légère · Structurée · Complexe

👃

Nez

The nose — surface polish: README, licence, CI, topics, description.

Fermé · Discret · Ouvert · Expressif

👄

Bouche

The palate — recent commits, contributors, issue health, releases.

Plat · Souple · Charnu · Puissant

🏁

Finale

The finish — stars, forks, watchers and how fast the stars pour in.

Courte · Moyenne · Longue · Persistante

🏆

Note finale

A weighted score out of 100 — Nez 20, Bouche 40, Finale 40.

Piquette · Correct · Bon cru · Grand cru
